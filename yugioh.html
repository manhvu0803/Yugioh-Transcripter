Hootcake
If this card in your possession is destroyed by your opponent's card and sent to your GY: Shuffle this card into the Deck. You can target 1 monster in your GY; banish it, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Madolche" monster from your Deck, except "Madolche Hootcake". You can only use this effect of "Madolche Hootcake" once per turn.
